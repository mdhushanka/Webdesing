Chicken Noodle Soup

Whether you're feeling under the weather or you're just in need of a little comfort, there's nothing like homemade chicken noodle soup to warm you up from the inside out. With this shortcut recipe, you can have the best chicken noodle soup of your life on the table in just 40 minutes.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

1. In a large pot, saut√© chopped onions, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened.

2. Add chicken broth, diced cooked chicken, and bring to a simmer.

3. Stir in egg noodles and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot. Enjoy your comforting chicken noodle soup!

ingredients

1 lb chicken, cooked and shredded

8 cups chicken broth

1 cup carrots, diced

1 cup celery, diced

1 cup onion, chopped

2 cups egg noodles

2 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley for garnish
